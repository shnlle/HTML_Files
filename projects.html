Project 2: Instagram

A social media platform designed to allow users to upload and share photos with their followers. This project utilizes HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP for its functionality. Instagram is a free photo and video sharing app available on iPhone and Android. People can upload photos or videos to our service and share them with their followers or with a select group of friends. They can also view, comment and like posts shared by their friends on Instagram. Anyone 13 and older can create an account by registering an email address and selecting a username.

Project 3: Anix

Anix is a game-changing platform for anime enthusiasts, providing a simple and minimal interface akin to AnimixPlay, alongside an array of additional features. With its responsive design, Myanimelist import/export capabilities, multiple fast streaming servers, hourly content updates, notification system, and synchronous access across devices, Anix offers an unparalleled streaming experience.
